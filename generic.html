The History of CMC Coffee Shop

"CMC Coffee Shop" began its journey in (2001), fueled by a passion for great coffee and creating a cozy space where people could come together. What started as a small, humble café in Intramuros "the heart of Manila" has grown into a community hub for coffee enthusiasts, students, professionals, and families. Our story began with a simple vision: to offer high-quality coffee, freshly brewed every day, paired with delicious treats in a welcoming environment. The name CMC reflects the initials of our founders, whose shared love for coffee culture inspired them to bring their dream to life.

From the beginning, we focused on:

• Quality Ingredients: Sourcing beans from local farmers and sustainable suppliers.

• Craftsmanship: Perfecting the art of brewing coffee to bring out its rich and distinct flavors.

• Craftsmanship: Building relationships with our customers and becoming a part of their daily lives.

Over the years, CMC Coffee Shop has evolved to meet the needs of its growing clientele. We've introduced a diverse menu of specialty drinks, signature desserts, and savory options to cater to every palate. Our café has also become a favorite spot for events, meetings, and study sessions, thanks to its warm ambiance and free Wi-Fi.

Today, CMC Coffee Shop is proud to continue serving the community with the same passion and commitment that started it all. Whether you're here for a quick caffeine fix or a long, relaxing stay, you're always welcome at CMC Coffee Shop – where every cup tells a story.
